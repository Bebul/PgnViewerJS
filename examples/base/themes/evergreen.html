The Evergreen Game

The following game is one of the most prominent in the history of chess. Play through the beautiful moves, and see where black is without and chance.

Try to use the play button, because the three boards have differenct speed defined. You can stop at any time, and use instead the cursor LEFT and RIGHT for playing back and forth. And you may switch colors during the play at will.
The first 2 button sets are the usual images displayed (in spans, not in buttons), the third one uses the standard font for displaying images on the buttons.
